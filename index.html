About Me

I’m Skyle Abayon, an undergraduate BSIT student at Cebu Technological University – Consolacion Campus. I’m passionate about graphic design and front-end web development. I enjoy crafting clean, creative, and user-friendly designs using tools like Canva and Figma.

Currently, I’m expanding my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks as I work toward becoming a full-stack web developer. I’m eager to collaborate on projects that combine design and technology to create meaningful digital experiences.

Outside of tech, I love exploring new ideas, learning through challenges, and continuously improving both my creativity and problem-solving skills. If you dont see me coding, you can see me playing my guitar, enjoying a walk, sketching something, making illustrations, browsing the internet, or sleeping.

Work

E-Commerce Webpage | Guitar Centrale

An E-commerce website for Musical Instruments and Accessories. Made with the help of XAMPP. Core languages used: HTML, CSS, JS, and PHP. Uses MySQL Database. Functionalities include: User authorization, User Roles (Admins and General Users), Manageable Item Catalogue (Add, Edit, and Delete), etc.

This project not only allowed me to gain hands-on experience with SQL operations, but also provided an opportunity to explore the full development lifecycle, from design to demonstration.

AABM | Inventory Management System

An Inventory Management System for theoretical implementation for multi-industrial purposes. Made using ASP.NET MVC Core. Honestly, I'd say that this was 98% developed by AI tools because this was a rushed school project together with its manuscripts too. I guess I got away with it..

The Telecaster Pubmat

Made with Canva. Highlights the versatility and beauty of the Fender Telecaster electric guitar. This pubmat was made just for fun and I felt the need to glaze on the Tele for a full hour.

Band Album Cover

Gig Calendar
